## Key Takeaways

- **Open to All**  
  The summer school is designed for anyone interested in Kubernetes, including platform engineers, SREs, and network professionals, regardless of their prior knowledge.

- **Four Sessions**  
  The program consists of four complementary sessions covering topics from load balancing to traffic management within Kubernetes environments.

- **Hands-On Experience**  
  Participants will engage in practical workshops, gaining hands-on experience deploying applications and load balancers in a guided environment.

- **Expert Guidance**  
  The sessions will include insights from experts, providing participants opportunities to ask questions and clarify complex topics.
